About me

I am the Group Production Director at award-winning women’s magazine Stylist.

As well as helping to develop Stylist’s unique tone of voice and overseeing the creation of the magazine from initial flatplan to finished printed product, I have grown the food content across different platforms, editing the Gourmet On-The-Go page, securing interviews with the likes of Yotam Ottolenghi and Anna Jones, writing for our email newsletter Stylist Loves and contributing to stylist.co.uk.

I write about all things food and drink, from gourmet crumpets to sexism in the beer industry.

I have also hosted sessions at our annual Stylist Live event, and have worked on commercial collaborations with brands such as Clarins, Dyson and Virgin Trains.
